The **expected value** of $X$ can be denoted as $E[X], \mu$,or $\mu_x$ and is given by
$$E[X]=\mu=\mu_X=\sum_{\{all~x\}}x\cdot f(x)$$
where $X$ is a **discrete** random variable with [[Probability Mass Function|PMF]] $f(x)$

For **continuous** random values with [[Probability Density Function|PDF]] $f(x)$
$$E[X]=\mu=\mu_X=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}x\cdot f(x)dx$$
